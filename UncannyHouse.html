Welcome to Uncanny House

A Rare Opportunity Waits

Important Features

  • Creepy Architecture: High ceilings, complicated wood designs, chandeliers from old times flickering in an odd manner.
  • Hidden Corners: Secret rooms behind bookshelves, a bizarre basement with a locked iron door, and an attic with old remains of family belongings.
  • Best Charm: Peeling old wallpaper from the 1800s, as it is just so old; large fireplaces lighting themselves once in a while, twisting staircases seeming to talk going up.
  • Special Feature: A great broken mirror at the entrance said to show images from other times.

History of the House

“This house was constructed in 1892, and it has a rather mysterious past. The Lavete family lived here before they disappeared on a stormy night, leaving everything behind. Other owners have reported strange events: footsteps, cold drafts, and eerie music. Its reputation still, to this day, the house stands, waiting for new caretakers for its secrets.”

Neighborhood

"Hollow's End is a, quiet community fit for those who needs privacy. Locals claim to hear ghostly howls in the woods at nighttime. The market and several antique shops in the town square are old but ruined and full of strange items."

Outdoor Space

  • Front Yard: It has a huge oak tree at the front yard that has repeatedly been struck by lightning.
  • Backyard: At the back of the house, the yard is overgrown, a maze full of weeds, a fountain that does not work, and a swing with cracked seats which seemed to swing in the without wind.
  • Swing Set: And of course, there's a crumbling hut just right for nice gatherings if you dare.
